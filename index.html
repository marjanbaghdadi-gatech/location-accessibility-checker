What the AI Checks

  • Ramp presence or missing ramp
  • Potential threshold or step barriers
  • Door access and handle usability
  • Handrails or support features
  • Pathway width and obstacles
  • Clear route to the entrance
  • Likely barriers for wheelchair users and people with mobility impairments
